❓ How to Use EWS Tracker

A quick guide for Team Leads and Admins
📋 FOR TEAM LEADS

1. Sign Up & Get Approved

No email required — just a name, a username, and a password. New accounts wait for admin approval before they can log in.

  • 1Click Sign up on the login screen and fill in your name, username, and password.
  • 2You'll land on a Waiting for Approval screen — this is normal.
  • 3Once your admin approves you in the Users tab, log back in and you're in.

2. Log In to Your Tracker

Once approved, log in with your username and password. You land straight on My Team — your own employee roster, private to you.

  • 1Your tabs are: My Team, Headcount, Permanent Attrition, and Settings.
  • 2Stat cards at the top always show your live counts — headcount and risk levels.
  • 3Nobody else's team is visible here — only your admin can see across teams.

3. Add an Employee

Click ＋ Add Employee, fill in the basics, and flip each warning indicator to YES or NO. The risk score and status calculate live as you go.

  • 1Enter Employee Name and Employee ID — the only two required fields.
  • 2Toggle the 10 warning indicators (tardiness, absences, low productivity, and so on).
  • 3Set With Active CAP?, add a rating and TL notes, then hit Save.

4. Understanding Risk Status

The risk score is the number of indicators marked YES, plus one if Active CAP is YES. Status follows the score automatically — you never set it manually.

🟢 GREEN — Score 0 🟡 YELLOW — Score 1–3 🔴 RED — Score 4+ ⚫ BLACK — Confirmed attrition

5. Bulk Import via Excel

Onboarding a whole roster? Skip the one-by-one form and use the spreadsheet template instead.

  • 1Click 📥 Download Template — a ready-made .xlsx with every column and a sample row.
  • 2Fill it out in Excel, one employee per row.
  • 3Click 📤 Upload Filled Excel — every valid row is added instantly.

6. Mark Permanent Attrition

When someone resigns or is terminated, don't delete them — confirm their attrition and they move themselves to a separate register.

  • 1Edit the employee and set Confirmed Attrition? to Yes.
  • 2An Attrition Date field appears — set it (defaults to today).
  • 3Save — they leave your active roster and appear in Permanent Attrition instead. Change it back to "No" any time to restore them.

7. Track Monthly Headcount

The Headcount tab tracks hires, transfers, and attrition month by month — Closing HC calculates itself and rolls into next month's Opening HC.

  • 1Click ＋ Add Monthly Entry, pick the month, enter hires/transfers/attrition.
  • 2Leave Opening HC blank to auto-carry last month's closing number.
  • 3YTD stat cards update instantly — hires, attrition, projected year-end headcount.

8. Search, Edit & Export

Small tools that save time once your roster grows.

  • 1The search box filters by name, ID, or position as you type.
  • 2✏️ edits an entry, 🗑 removes it — changes save to the cloud immediately.
  • 3⬇ Export CSV downloads your current roster for reporting or offline review.
⚙ FOR ADMINS

9. Your Dashboard

Admins log in to a bird's-eye view of every team — total headcount, risk breakdown, and a per-team summary table.

  • 1Top stat cards total every approved team's active roster.
  • 2Team-Level Risk Summary ranks each team lead's counts, avg score, and action priority.
  • 3View Team → opens that team's full tracker, read-only.

10. Click Any Number to Drill In

Every stat card on the Dashboard is clickable — no need to open each team one by one to find who's driving a number.

  • 1Click 🔴 Red and see every red-flagged employee across all teams at once.
  • 2Click ⚫ Permanent Attrition to jump straight into the full attrition register.
  • 3Each drilldown has its own search box and CSV export.

11. View Any Team's Full Roster

Need the complete picture for one team lead? Click View Team → from the Dashboard table to open their entire tracker in read-only mode.

  • 1See every column their team lead sees — indicators, notes, ratings.
  • 2No edit/delete controls here — admins view, team leads maintain.
  • 3Use ← Back to Dashboard to return to the overview.

12. Headcount & Attrition, All Teams

Both tabs work the same way for admins as for team leads — except you can view the combined total or filter down to a single team.

  • 1Headcount: pick "Overall — All Teams" or any single team from the dropdown.
  • 2Permanent Attrition: filter by team and by month to review confirmed exits.
  • 3Both are read-only for admins and export to CSV.

13. Performer — Who's Behind on Updates

The 🚦 Performer tab flags team leads whose average last-update falls outside a healthy 5–12 day window, so nothing goes stale unnoticed.

  • 1🔴 Overdue — average update older than 12 days.
  • 2🟢 Fresh — average update younger than 5 days (also worth a look).
  • 3View Team → jumps straight to the team behind the number.

14. Approve New Users

New signups can't log in until you say so. The Users tab is where every account gets approved, revoked, or removed.

  • 1Pending signups show a PENDING badge — click ✓ Approve to activate them.
  • 2Revoke access locks an approved account back out without deleting their data.
  • 3Remove permanently deletes the account and their employee records.
